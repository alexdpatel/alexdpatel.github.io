Automatically a new sitemap uploaded every 14~21 days

A new service: instead of logging in to FreeSitemapGenerator.com and manually click the Make Sitemap button, we can do it for you. A brand new sitemap will be generated for you every 14 days and in combination with the auto upload package it will be installed on your site. This feature is only available for our Premium Membership users.

How it works: every 14 to 21 days the selected websites will be queued to start a new sitemap creation process, as if you would have manually clicked on the Create Sitemap button.
